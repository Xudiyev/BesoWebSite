About Beso App

In Azerbaijan, 45% of the economy is SMEs, of which 85% are natural persons, of which a majority have not yet adopted any type of technology or digital tool. We are talking about millions of businesses and entrepreneurs that are being left out of the digital economy!

We cannot imagine a Azerbaijan without its sole proprietors, small and micro businesses. At Beso seek to provide them with the necessary tools to help them control their finances, make better decisions, that in turn offers them access to tools to grow their business.

We cannot imagine Azerbaijan without its entrepreneurs. We are here to be your ally.
